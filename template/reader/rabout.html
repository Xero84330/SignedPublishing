About Us

Founded in 2023, our private company is located in Coldwater, Mississippi, operating with a small team of dedicated members. Despite our size, we aim to make a significant impact. We foster innovation and provide exceptional services and products. Being privately held allows. As a new player in the market, we are poised to establish ourselves and build a strong reputation through quality and commitment.

Our Vision

In our envisioned future, self-publishing is no longer a last resort fraught with uncertainty and potential failure. Instead, we aspire to create an environment where authors are equipped with choices and a distinct voice, allowing them to publish with confidence and achieve success on their terms. This world will eliminate the risks and challenges that often accompany the publishing journey, fostering a supportive ecosystem that encourages creativity and passion. Authors will be empowered to pursue their dreams without the burdens associated with traditional publishing models, nurturing their unique stories and connecting with readers in meaningful ways. By providing innovative tools and resources, we aim to transform the publishing landscape into a thriving avenue for every writer to flourish.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform the publishing landscape by introducing fair and transparent contracts that prioritize the welfare of authors. We stand firmly against predatory practices that have long plagued the industry, advocating for a new standard where authors receive a significantly increased royalty rate of 80%. By doing so, we aim to empower writers, enabling them to thrive from their creative works rather than being exploited. Our commitment is not only to enhance authors' earnings but also to foster a culture of respect and collaboration within the publishing world. Through these changes, we envision a more equitable industry where every voice is valued and every story has the opportunity to shine. By leading this charge, we hope to inspire other publishers to rethink their approaches and ultimately elevate the standards of publishing for all.
